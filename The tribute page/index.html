About Shah Rukh Khan



SRK or Shah Rukh Khan, (born on November 2, 1965, in New Delhi, India. [now Mumbai]), is a renowned Indian actor, film producer, and television personality. He is one of the most popular and influential actors in the Indian film industry and has acted in numerous blockbuster movies in a career spanning over three decades. SRK is also known as the "King of Bollywood" due to his immense popularity and contribution to the Indian cinema. He has won numerous awards for his acting performances, including 14 Filmfare Awards, which is the highest number of awards won by any actor. He is also a successful film producer and has produced several critically acclaimed movies under his production company, Red Chillies Entertainment. SRK is also known for his philanthropic work and is actively involved in various social causes such as child health and education, disaster relief, and women's empowerment.
